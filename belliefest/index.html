Camp Hostýn and Accommodations

We'll be at Camp Hostýn all weekend, and we have space for you to stay here, for free! If you're invited to Belliefest, we have a bed for you at Camp Hostýn for one or both nights! We'd love for you to stay with us on site for the weekend.

Camp Hostýn

Cabins sleep 3 people each. If you RSVP indicating you're staying with us, you'll get a cabin! You won't have to share with anyone outside your party, unless you want to. You are also welcome to tent on the property, if you prefer.

Cabins have mattresses but not bedding. If you can, please bring warm bedding (September in QC can get quite cold, and the cabins are not insulated)! If you're coming from afar or can't bring some yourself, just let us know and we'll bring some for you.

There is no electricity in the cabins, but it is available in the other buildings at Hostýn. You'll be able to charge your devices during the day if needed.

Hostýn has one shared, all-gender bathhouse with toilets and showers in individual stalls.

Camp Hostýn packing list:

In addition to your clothes, toiletries, and other personal items, please bring:

  • Headlamp/flashlight. No electricity in the cabins or lights on the way to the bathhouse, so you'll need it to see.
  • Bedding (sleeping bag or linens). The cabins have bare mattresses. If you're coming from afar or can't bring bedding, no problem! Just let us know, we'll bring ya some.
  • Bathing suit. We'll be swimming Saturday morning, but feel free to hit the water any time.
  • Warm layers. We're going to be in non-insulated buildings in Quebec in September, please dress accordingly, and bring extra layers!
  • Shower sandles.
  • Snacks, if you want! We'll be providing all (vegetarian) meals.

Other accommodation options:

Don't want to rough it with us? That's fine! We will be located in Saint-Calixte, QC. Here are some local options:

  • A chalet [recommended]: The surrounding area is ~chalet country~ and there are many rental properties and "glamping" opportunities close by. Check out Google Maps and Airbnb.
  • Hôtel le Riviera, Rawdon QC (~20 min drive): https://ca.hotels.com/ho3725965408/hotel-le-riviera/
  • Rawdon Golf Resort Hotel, Rawdon, QC (~22 min drive): https://rawdongolfresort.com/en/hotel/rates
  • Anywhere in Montreal: Montreal is about a 1.5h drive from Camp Hostýn. If you want to use the weekend to explore the city, you can drive up to Saint-Calixte for the day and return to the city after dinner.

Other Info + FAQ

On food:

We will be providing vegetarian meals and snacks from Friday evening to Sunday morning, with gluten free and vegan options available for those who need. If you have specific dietary restrictions, please let us know and we'll work with you to figure out how to keep you fed.

On gifts:

Over the last 8 years together, we have built a beautiful life and don't need anything more. Your presence and warmth celebrating with us is the only gift we need. If you feel moved to do something more, we would love a family recipe, a shared memory, or a handmade item.

On dress code:

Dress in something that you'd like to be photographed in! We're going to keep it dressy casual for the ceremony. If weather permits, there will be some dancing outside, so make sure your footwear can survive a bit of dirt and grass.

Can I come for just the ceremony?

Of course, kiddo! Come any time before 3:30pm on Saturday to make the ceremony, and leave whenever you get sick of us 😎
